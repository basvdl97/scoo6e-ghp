001 — about

Irish-Dutch by blood. Amsterdam to London by way of pandemic, late nights, and a refusal to do anything halfway.

ScoobE has been chasing this since the start — first EP cut with SDK, somewhere north of five million streams later, a catalog that moves between melancholy and motion without pretending the two don't live in the same house.

Collabs along the way with Hare Squead, Skizzy Mars, Alaina Castillo, All Saints, problem solved, and a long list of others. The sound: warm, lived-in, honest about the mess. Influence pulled openly from Mac Miller's later years — the cracked-open vulnerability, the way a beat can carry weight without raising its voice.

Latest: hard2handle. — out now.
